CREATING OPPORTUNITIES THROUGH ACHIEVEMENT

NAIS Dublin is Ireland’s first and only school offering the IB curriculum from age 3-18. We’re proud to be developing a new generation of curious, wise, ambitious young people who are motivated to succeed, no matter what path they take in life.

We offer a truly global experience with outstanding learning opportunities and world-class teachers. Every one of our students has the opportunity to shine, to realise their talents and make a real difference in a world beyond the classroom.

In addition to an outstanding co-curricular offering, Nord Anglia Education's global collaborations with world-leading organisations like MIT, The Juilliard School and UNICEF enhance your child’s learning.
